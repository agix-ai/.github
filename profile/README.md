**An operating system for teams of AI agents you own and run yourself.**

Most AI tools rent you a single assistant. Agix installs a **governed, legible fleet** of specialist agents on infrastructure *you* control — so you can ship like a team of twenty and own your whole stack.

### Why it's different

- **You own it.** Runs on your machine and your infrastructure — no API key required to start. Your data, your agents, your knowledge never leave your control.
- **It's governed.** Every agent's work is checked by a *separate* agent, and every action traces back to a person. A fleet you can actually trust with real work — not a black box.
- **It compounds.** The hive keeps what it learns in a knowledge graph you own. The longer it works alongside you, the more it knows your domain — and the sharper and cheaper it gets over time.

### Two ways in

- **Agix AOS** — the open-source core. A local agentic operating system: install your environment and a team of specialist agents, then build toward your north star. Runs on your machine, no API key required. *(Open-sourcing now.)*
- **Agix** — agentic implementation work, for teams that want the foundation designed and built *with* them.

Built on a foundation you own and grow — not features you rent.
